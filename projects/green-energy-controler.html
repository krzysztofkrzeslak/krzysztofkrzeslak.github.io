Green Energy Controller

Date: 2023

The Green Energy Controller project focuses on the development of software and hardware for a GSM network-connected controller. This device is designed for power monitoring applications, enabling efficient and reliable energy management. The controller integrates advanced communication technologies such as LTE and GSM, ensuring seamless connectivity for real-time data transmission and monitoring.

My key contributions to this project include designing the software architecture and hardware components, ensuring optimal performance and reliability. The project also involved leveraging Linux-based systems for robust and scalable solutions.

Technologies Used

  • Linux
  • LTE
  • GSM
  • Hardware Design
